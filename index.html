Verified Origin Protocol

Verify where content comes from — not what it looks like.

VOP is a neutral protocol designed to verify the declared origin of digital content in a world where visual authenticity can no longer be trusted.

What VOP does

  • Records the cryptographic fingerprint of digital content
  • Associates it with a declared origin and timestamp
  • Provides a public, verifiable proof page

What VOP does NOT do

  • Does not detect AI-generated content
  • Does not judge truth or authenticity
  • Does not store or analyze media files

VOP answers one question:

Does this content have a verifiable origin?
